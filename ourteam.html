Opulent Vision is made up of three young florists who united under a common goal of bringing the floral industry into the 21st century. Anna and Liubov are a sister and sister-in-law pair who joined together to pursue education and training in wedding floristry and event decoration at the Moscow Flower School in Russia. Also working to curate bespoke prop pieces that are manufactured in-house, there’s not much the duo aren’t willing to take on together. After completing their training, the pair set off on a trip to Portugal to begin putting their newfound knowledge and training into practice.

Anna and Liubov recently completed an event designer course in the Moscow Flower School, also pursuing her Diploma in Wedding Planning, Styling, and Design at the Arabian Academy of Wedding & Event Planning.

As the pair continued to work as florists for large events like weddings and opulent birthday parties, they met a Senior Florist by the name of Yana. After working with Yana and discussing their potential for a three-florist team, Yana decided to join Opulent Vision in a full-time role.

Today. Opulent Vision is a team of three young creative enthusiasts looking to take the world of floral and event design by storm. With over 7 years of combined experience, we’re ready to bring cutting-edge and innovative event design and floral decor solutions to your next wedding, event, birthday party, or styled photo session.
